ABOUT US

Welcome to POEM AND POETRY FOR KIDS, your number one source for all things [product]. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of "POEM and STORY", with an emphasis on Information For Child, Motivate, Happy Mood.

Founded in 2022 by Abrar ul Hassan, POEM AND POETRY FOR KIDS has come a long way from its beginnings in Korangi Crossing Karachi Pakistan. When Abrar ul Hassan first started out, his passion for Writing POETRY and Story drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
